Policies & Terms

Transparency and trust are important to us. Please review our policies carefully before placing an order.

Privacy Policy

We collect only essential information required to process your order and provide customer support.

  • Payment confirmation (handled securely by third-party gateways)

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with unauthorized parties.

Payment Policy

  • All payments must be completed at checkout
  • Prices are listed in local currency
  • Orders are processed only after payment confirmation

We reserve the right to cancel orders due to payment failure or suspicious activity.

Shipping Policy

  • Processing Time: 3–7 business days
  • Shipping Time: 5–10 business days (location dependent)
  • Tracking details are shared after dispatch

Delays caused by customs, weather, or courier services are beyond our control.

Return & Exchange Policy

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Custom or personalized jewellery is non-returnable

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders may be canceled within 24 hours of placement
  • Cancellations are not guaranteed once production begins

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days.

Custom & Handmade Variations

Our jewellery is handcrafted. Minor variations in texture, finish, or tone are natural and not considered defects.

Product Care

  • Avoid water, perfume, and chemicals
  • Store in a dry, soft-lined box
  • Clean gently with a soft cloth

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours with clear photos of the issue.
